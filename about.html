Who we are

Teles Technological Services LLP was born from a simple yet powerful idea: that technology should be more than just a tool—it should be a catalyst for transformation. Founded by a group of passionate technologists and problem-solvers, Teles Tech started with a shared vision of creating solutions that not only meet the needs of businesses today but also anticipate the challenges of tomorrow.

It all began in a small, vibrant room filled with whiteboards, laptops, and a team of innovators driven by the desire to make a difference. Each of us brought something unique to the table—whether it was expertise in software development, a deep understanding of data engineering, or a knack for project management. What united us was our unwavering commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible with technology.
